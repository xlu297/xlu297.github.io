Peking Duck

Roast Peking Duck from Red House Spice

Peking Duck is an iconic Chinese dish featuring crispy roast duck served with pancakes, scallions, and hoisin sauce. I love the contrast between the crisp skin and the soft wrap, and the bold savory flavor makes it one of my all-time favorites.

Sweet Lotus Root With Sticky Rice

Lotus Root with Sticky Rice from Red House Spice

This is a traditional Chinese dessert made by stuffing sweet glutinous rice into slices of lotus root, then simmering it in a fragrant syrup. I love how the chewy texture of the rice contrasts with the natural crunch of the lotus root. It’s comforting, slightly floral, and not overly sweet—perfect as a light dessert or afternoon treat.
